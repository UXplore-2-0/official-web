Adding more members to your team not only ensures your team's eligibility but also enhances collaboration and diversity of perspectives, which are valuable assets in such events.

Here are the steps to add members to your team:
  1. Log in to your team’s account by clicking the “Log in” button on the website.
  2. Once logged in, navigate to the "Add Members" sidebar tab.
  3. Click on the "Add Member" button.
  4. Enter the details of the member you’re adding, and click the “Add Member” button there to confirm the addition.
  5. Repeat this procedure separately for each team member, including the team leader.
